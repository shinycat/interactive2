Emoji University has created a demanding, extensive and competitive program. In order to follow in the footsteps of the origianl creater, Kurita, we have implemented his system of design to pass down to the best and the brightest. All areas will be studied and critiqued extensively, such as color, shapes and pyschology. Emoji communication begins from day one. Students and professors communicate strictly with emojis.

WHY

It's the future, maan. We got shit to do. There's no time for face to face conversation. Send a few emoji's and seal that business deal. Send a "Will you marry me?" text with a heart emoji border, and you'll have a partner for life! No doubt. The world needs designers skilled and dedicated to create the evergrowing form of communication. Emojis. All day everyday. As the technology takes us to new heights, it is important to have the best and brightest thinkers of the taking our daily expressions and morphing them into a teeny weeny little yellow round faces. Emojis are quick and straight to the point and nothing gets lost in translation.
